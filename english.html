Shruti Sankalanam is a tool to digitize your spiritual scriptures and add pitch (tone marks) to them. It is very easy to use, just follow these 5 steps:

  1. Create a Book and Section: Give a name to the scripture you want to digitize (Example: "Scripture"). Then create a section inside it (Example: "Introduction").
  2. Upload Image and Extract Text: Upload an image containing the mantra from your phone or computer. Click the 'Start OCR' button. It will recognize characters from the image and provide you text.
  3. Save the Text: Give a name and description to your mantra, then click the 'Save New Mantra' button.
  4. View and Download Text: You can view saved texts anytime. Once done, click the 'Download Book' button to save the entire book as an HTML file.
